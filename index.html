704 Cocktail Menu*

*All measurements are posted in ounces (oz) for 1 drink, scale accordingly for multiple drinks

Wake up!!

Espresso Martini - Classic

  • 2 oz Vodka
  • 1/2 oz Coffee liqueur
  • 1 oz Espresso
  • 1/2 oz Simple syrup
  • Add ingredients in a cocktail mixer over ice and stir for 15-20 seconds, double-strain and serve

Espresso Martini - Tequila

  • 1.5 oz Tequila Blanco
  • 1 oz Coffee Liqueur
  • 1/2 oz Licor 43
  • 2 oz Espresso
  • Add ingredients in a cocktail mixer over ice and stir for 15-20 seconds, double-strain and serve

Espresso Martini - Decadent

  • 1.5 oz Baileys Chocolate Liqueur
  • 0.75 oz Vodka
  • 1 oz Fresh Espresso
  • 0.25 oz Simple Syrup
  • Add ingredients in a cocktail mixer over ice and stir for 15-20 seconds, double-strain and serve

Carajillo

  • 1.5 oz Licor 43
  • 1.5 oz Fresh Espresso
  • Add ingredients in a cocktail mixer over ice and stir for 15-20 seconds, double-strain and serve

Fancy a drink?

Negroni

  • 1 oz Gin
  • 1 oz Campari
  • 1 oz Sweet Vermouth
  • Orange slice
  • Add ingredients directly in glass, stir gently for 5-10 seconds over ice

Mojito

  • 5 mint leaves
  • 1.5 oz white rum
  • 3/4 oz lime juice
  • 3/4 oz simple syrup
  • 1.5 oz club soda
  • Add ingredients directly in glass, stir gently for 5-10 seconds over ice

Paloma

  • 2 oz tequila blanco or mezcal
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • 4 oz grapefruit soda
  • Add ingredients directly in glass, stir gently for 5-10 seconds over ice

Spanish Gin Tonic

  • 2 oz gin
  • 4 oz tonic water
  • Lemon wheel, Thyme and Juniper berries for garnish
  • Add ingredients directly in glass, stir gently for 5-10 seconds over ice

Shaky-Shaky!!

Extra-dirty Dry Martini

  • 2 oz Gin (Bombay)
  • 1/4 oz Vermouth
  • 1 oz olive brine
  • Add ingredients in a cocktail mixer over ice and stir for 15-20 seconds, double-strain and serve

Classical Martini

  • 2.5 oz Gin
  • 1/2 oz Vermouth
  • 1 oz olive brine
  • Add ingredients in a cocktail mixer over ice and stir for 15-20 seconds, double-strain and serve

Peruvian Pisco Sour

  • 2 oz Pisco
  • 1/2 oz lemon juice
  • 1/2 oz lime juice
  • 3/4 oz simple syrup
  • 1 egg white
  • Add ingredients in a cocktail mixer and stir for 15-20 seconds without ice, then add ice and shake for 15-20 seconds, finally double-strain and serve
  • 3 drops angostura bitters for the finishing touch

Margarita

  • 1.5 oz tequila blanco or mezcal
  • 1 oz Cointreau
  • 3/4 oz lime juice
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup (optional)
  • Add ingredients in a cocktail mixer over ice and stir for 15-20 seconds, serve in glass with ice

Pina Colada Malibu

  • 1 oz Malibu
  • 1.5 oz pineapple juice
  • 1/2 oz coconut cream
  • Add ingredients in a cocktail mixer over ice and stir for 15-20 seconds, serve in glass with ice

El George

  • 2 oz Scotch
  • 3/4 oz sweet vermouth
  • 1/2 oz Benedictine
  • Add ingredients in a cocktail mixer over ice and stir for 15-20 seconds, double-strain and serve
  • Lemon peel (yellow)
